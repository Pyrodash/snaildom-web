Snaildom was created to be an exciting, original and different place for kids to hang out in a safe online playground. With young people in mind, a carefully crafted virtual forest has been constructed for safe chat, games, quests, creativity and more being developed all the time.

This brand new virtual world aims to make a perfect home for our already existent community, the DamenSpike GAMES HQ, and any other new comers who are interested in joining in the fun... which so many children around the world are so passionate about and have been loyal to for the past years.
